I Am Jared Lewis

Highschool Student

Hello, my name is Jared Lewis. I am currently a junior attending Arsenal Technical High School. I have a wide range of aspirations, from robotics and coding to playing soccer and representing my country in the World Cup. However, after much consideration and keeping note I still have a lot of time, I have narrowed down my focus to developing, building, and programming robots. My goal is to create robots that make a meaningful impact on people's lives. Whether it's something small, like building fully autonomous public transportation buses to improve one'sdaily commutes, or something more significant, like developing robotic body parts to help individuals regain normal function after a tragic loss of theirs. In addition to planning for my future, I am actively involved in extracurricular activities, including robotics clubs and soccer teams, and I have participated in numerous camps and internships. For more information about me, please visit the "About" section.
